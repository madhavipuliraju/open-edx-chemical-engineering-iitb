THEORY

Vapour pressure is the pressure of a vapour that is in equilibrium with its liquid phase in a closed system. The vapour pressure of a liquid is the indication of a liquid’s evaporation rate.

 

A common relation between the vapour pressure and temperature is the Antoine equation. Using this equation, the vapour pressure of a species can be calculated given the temperature and vice versa. This is very useful in chemical industries, since operations like distillation require us to know the vapour pressure of the various components as the operating temperature.
