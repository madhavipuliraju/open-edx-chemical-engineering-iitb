PROCEDURE

1) Sample of the liquid is filled in ebulliometer.

 

2) Make the initial pressure atmospheric by opening the pressure valve and then closing it again.

 

3) Start the heater and temperature sensor.

 

4) Vapour form and rise in the tube to the flask chamber.

 

5) Vapour moves through thermo wall and then condenses.

 

6) Allow the system to reach steady state.

 

7) Observe the drop rate and adjust the rheostat such that the drop rate should be between 40 and 90 per minute.

 

8) Take two reading with slight change in the rheostat.

 

9) Change the pressure using vacuum pump and again do the same procedure.

 

10) Take atleast six readings.
